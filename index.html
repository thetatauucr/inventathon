Inventathon

The Inventathon is the first quarter long hackathon in Southern California. We invite students of all disciplines to bring their ideas and to create something really awesome. Unlike a traditional hackathon, the Inventathon allows participants to work on their project throughout the quarter instead of a designated weekend. Join us in 8 weeks of innovation.
